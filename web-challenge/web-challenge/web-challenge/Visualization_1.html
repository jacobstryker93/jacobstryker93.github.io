Stellar Mass

This graph will show the relationship between stellar mass of a star and the amount of planets said star can support.
The data shows that there are few relationships between how much mass a star has and how many planets it can support.
There is an observable "sweet spot" in the middle masses however which is indicative of our own star "the Sun".
