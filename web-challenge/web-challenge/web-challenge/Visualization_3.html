Stellar Density

This graph will show the relationship between stellar density of a star and the amount of planets said star can support.
The data shows that there are few relationships between how much density a star has and how many planets it can support.
It is interesting that the cluster of density levels towards the center can support 8 planets and that that is not seen anywhere else.
This is in line with our solar system's star's density and that it supports more than 7 planets.
